About

Carlos Manuel Cejas

I was born and raised in Philadelphia, the son of two Cuban immigrants. Now I study computer science at Trinity College Dublin.

A lot of people saw moving from the US to Dublin for CS as a step down. I saw a market I wanted to be in: a growing tech scene, serious builders, and a place where being American is still unusual enough to open doors. I did not just pick a school. I picked a market where I could have an edge.
